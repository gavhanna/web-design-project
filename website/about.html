Get to know us!

Founded in 2007, Luigi's prides itself on bringing an authentic Italian experience to you. All our food is handmade and fresh daily. Our recipies go back centuries !


Situated in the heart of Dublin City, Luigi's offers you the very best of Italian cuisine.


Luigi's has picked up the James Beard award(2008), The Luna Award(2009) and the Italia del Gusto (2012).





Our Executive Chef is the legendary Michael Corleone. Born in Trapani, Sicily, Michael brings to you an incredible Italian menu, that you will never forget.


Come inside and enjoy authentic dishes and genuine welcoming Italian service, or just sit with a glass of wine and a plate of antipasti and watch the world go by.


We look forward to welcoming you very soon!
